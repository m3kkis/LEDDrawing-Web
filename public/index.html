LED CONTROLLER

Little project where you can draw something on the board below and it will appear on my LED frame at home.

UPCOMING FEATURE: Display a photo of my frame with your drawing on it after clicking submit.(Edit: I just realised my camera module cable doesn't fit the Pi Zero, so maybe I'll get a new one or I'll just drop this idea.)

HOW-TO

  1. Select a color in the little box.
  2. Click once on the little circle to add the color
  3. Click a second time on the same circle to remove the color
  4. Click SUBMIT to send the drawing to my LED Frame
  5. Click RESET to clear all of the circles
